Long-tailed Tit

銀喉長尾山雀 · 银喉长尾山雀 · 銀喉長尾山雀 · Mésange à longue queue · Mito común

Aegithalos caudatus

A tiny pink, white and black ball of fluff with a very long tail. They travel in chattering family groups through the trees.
