Western Jackdaw

寒鴉 · 寒鸦 · 寒鴉 · Choucas des tours · Grajilla occidental

Coloeus monedula

A small smart crow with sparkling pale-grey eyes. Jackdaws form lifelong pairs and remember faces — they know who feeds them!

Size
Medium · 34 cm
Latin name
Coloeus monedula
Where to spot
  • Towns
  • Old buildings & church towers
  • Farmland
  • Cliffs

Where you can spot the Western Jackdaw in Ireland

  • Phoenix Park — Dublin: One of the biggest city parks in Europe. Ducks on the ponds, robins in the trees, magpies on the lawns — and a herd of fallow deer too!
