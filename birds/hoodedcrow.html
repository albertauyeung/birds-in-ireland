Hooded Crow

冠小嘴烏鴉 · 冠小嘴乌鸦 · 冠小嘴烏鴉 · Corneille mantelée · Corneja cenicienta

Corvus cornix

A clever grey-and-black crow seen everywhere in Ireland. Crows can solve puzzles to get food and even use sticks as tools.

Size
Medium · 50 cm
