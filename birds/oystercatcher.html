Eurasian Oystercatcher

蠣鷸 · 蛎鹬 · 蠣鷸 · Huîtrier pie · Ostrero euroasiático

Haematopus ostralegus

A striking black-and-white shorebird with a bright orange beak as long as its head. Its loud "pic, pic!" call is the soundtrack of Irish beaches.

Size
Medium · 43 cm
Latin name
Haematopus ostralegus
Where to spot
  • Beaches and rocky shores
  • Estuaries
  • All around the Irish coast
