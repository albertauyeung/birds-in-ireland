Long-eared Owl

長耳鴞 · 长耳鸮 · 長耳鴞 · Hibou moyen-duc · Búho chico

Asio otus

A slender owl with long "ears" (actually feather tufts) that point straight up when it's alert. Hunts mice in the dark.

Size
Medium · 36 cm
Latin name
Asio otus
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Conifer plantations
  • Woodlands
  • Hunts over fields and bogs at night
