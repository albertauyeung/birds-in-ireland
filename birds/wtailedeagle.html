White-tailed Eagle

白尾海鵰 · 白尾海雕 · 白尾海鵰 · Pygargue à queue blanche · Pigargo europeo

Haliaeetus albicilla

Ireland's biggest eagle, with wings up to 2.4 metres wide. Once gone from Ireland, eagles have been brought back since 2007.

Size
