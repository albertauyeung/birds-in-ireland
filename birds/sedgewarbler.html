Sedge Warbler

水蒲葦鶯 · 水蒲苇莺 · 水蒲葦鶯 · Phragmite des joncs · Carricerín común

Acrocephalus schoenobaenus

A streaky brown warbler with a creamy eyebrow. Males sing a wild, bubbly mix of squeaks and whistles from reedbeds — and never repeat the same song twice!

Size
Small · 13 cm
Latin name
Acrocephalus schoenobaenus
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Reedbeds
  • Wet bushes near water
  • Marshes (summer)
