Common Greenshank

青腳鷸 · 青脚鹬 · 青腳鷸 · Chevalier aboyeur · Archibebe claro

Tringa nebularia

A taller cousin of the redshank, with green-grey legs and an upturned beak. Listen for its musical "tew-tew-tew!" call.

Size
Medium · 32 cm
Latin name
Tringa nebularia
Where to spot
  • Estuaries and coastal pools
  • Lake shores (autumn)
  • Wexford Reserve
Learn more on Wikipedia →

Related birds

  • Common Sandpiper (Actitis hypoleucos)
  • Common Redshank (Tringa totanus)

Where you can spot the Common Greenshank in Ireland

  • Wexford Wildfowl Reserve — County Wexford: Around 250 species recorded here. In winter, thousands of geese and swans gather on the wet meadows.
