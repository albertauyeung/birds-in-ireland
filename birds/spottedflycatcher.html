Spotted Flycatcher

斑鶲 · 斑鹟 · 斑鶲 · Gobemouche gris · Papamoscas gris

Muscicapa striata

A grey-brown bird that sits very still on a perch, then SHOOTS out to grab a flying insect — and lands back on the same spot!

Size
Small · 14 cm
Latin name
Muscicapa striata
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Old gardens with mature trees
  • Woodland edges
  • Parks (summer)

Where you can spot the Spotted Flycatcher in Ireland

  • Phoenix Park — Dublin: One of the biggest city parks in Europe. Ducks on the ponds, robins in the trees, magpies on the lawns — and a herd of fallow deer too!
