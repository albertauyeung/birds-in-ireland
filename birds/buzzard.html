Common Buzzard

普通鵟 · 普通𫛢 · 普通鵟 · Buse variable · Busardo ratonero

Buteo buteo

A large brown bird of prey that soars in wide circles, calling "kee-yaw!" Buzzards have made an amazing comeback in Ireland.

Size
Large · 55 cm
Latin name
Buteo buteo
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Soaring high over woods, fields and hills
  • Now common across most of Ireland

Where you can spot the Common Buzzard in Ireland

  • Killarney National Park — County Kerry: Ancient oak woods and big lakes. Watch the sky for white-tailed eagles, brought back to Ireland from 2007. The oak woods buzz with songbirds.
