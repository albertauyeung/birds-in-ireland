Common Starling

紫翅椋鳥 · 紫翅椋鸟 · 紫翅椋鳥 · Étourneau sansonnet · Estornino pinto

Sturnus vulgaris

From far they look black, but up close their feathers shine with purple and green. At sunset, thousands swirl together in beautiful clouds called murmurations.

Size
Medium · 22 cm
Latin name
Sturnus vulgaris
Where to spot
  • Towns and gardens
  • Fields
  • Reedbeds at sunset (autumn murmurations)
