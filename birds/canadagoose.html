Canada Goose

加拿大雁 · 加拿大雁 · 加拿大雁 · Bernache du Canada · Barnacla canadiense

Branta canadensis

A big brown goose with a black head and neck and a bright white 'chinstrap'. They were brought from North America hundreds of years ago and now honk loudly on park lakes all over Ireland.

Size
Large · 95 cm
