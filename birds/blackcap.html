Eurasian Blackcap

黑頂林鶯 · 黑顶林莺 · 黑頂林鶯 · Fauvette à tête noire · Curruca capirotada

Sylvia atricapilla

A grey warbler whose beautiful song is sometimes called "the nightingale of Ireland". Males have a black cap, females a chestnut one!

Size
Small · 14 cm
Latin name
Sylvia atricapilla
Where to spot
  • Woodlands and gardens
  • Hedgerows (summer)
  • More gardens in winter
