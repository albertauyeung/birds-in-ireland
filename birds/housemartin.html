Common House Martin

白腹毛腳燕 · 白腹毛脚燕 · 白腹毛腳燕 · Hirondelle de fenêtre · Avión común

Delichon urbicum

A summer visitor that builds neat mud nests stuck under house roofs. Has a snowy white bottom and steel-blue back!

Size
Small · 14 cm
Latin name
Delichon urbicum
Where to spot
  • Town and village houses (under eaves)
  • Open countryside, summer
