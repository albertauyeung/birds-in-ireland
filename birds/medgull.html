Mediterranean Gull

地中海鷗 · 地中海鸥 · 地中海鷗 · Mouette mélanocéphale · Gaviota cabecinegra

Ichthyaetus melanocephalus

Looks like a black-headed gull but with a darker, fully black hood in summer. Called "the laughing gull" in many languages because of its yodelling call!

Size
Medium · 38 cm
Latin name
Ichthyaetus melanocephalus
Where to spot
  • Coasts and harbours
  • Coastal lakes (winter visitor)
