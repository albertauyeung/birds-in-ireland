Twite

黃嘴朱頂雀 · 黄嘴朱顶雀 · 黃嘴朱頂雀 · Linotte à bec jaune · Pardillo piquigualdo

Linaria flavirostris

Like the linnet's mountain cousin — streaky brown with a tiny yellow beak in winter. Lives in some of Ireland's wildest places.

Size
Small · 14 cm
Latin name
Linaria flavirostris
Where to spot
  • Mountains and uplands
  • Coastal saltmarshes (winter)
  • Mostly western Ireland
