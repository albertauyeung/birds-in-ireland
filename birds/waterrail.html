西方秧雞 · 西方秧鸡 · 西方秧雞 · Râle d'eau · Rascón europeo

Rallus aquaticus

A super shy bird that makes spooky pig-like "squealing" sounds from deep in the reeds. Heard far more often than seen!

Size
Small · 26 cm
Latin name
Rallus aquaticus
Where to spot
  • Reedbeds
  • Marshy edges of lakes and rivers
  • Wet boglands
