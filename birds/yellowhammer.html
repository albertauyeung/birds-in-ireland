Yellowhammer

黃鵐 · 黄鹀 · 黃鵐 · Bruant jaune · Escribano cerillo

Emberiza citrinella

A bright yellow bunting whose song sounds like "a little bit of bread and no cheese!" Sadly becoming rare on Irish farmland.

Size
Small · 16 cm
Latin name
Emberiza citrinella
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Hedgerows on tilled farmland
  • Especially east coast
