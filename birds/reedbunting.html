Reed Bunting

蘆鵐 · 芦鹀 · 蘆鵐 · Bruant des roseaux · Escribano palustre

Emberiza schoeniclus

A streaky brown bird that lives in reedbeds. Males in spring have a smart black head with a white moustache!

Size
Small · 15 cm
Latin name
Emberiza schoeniclus
Where to spot
  • Reedbeds
  • Wet bushy fields
  • Lake edges
