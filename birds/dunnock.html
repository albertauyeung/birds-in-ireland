Where you can spot the Dunnock in Ireland

  • Marlay Park — Dublin (Rathfarnham): A grand parkland estate in south Dublin, once the country home of the Le Touche banking family. Free-roaming peacocks call from the trees near the walled garden, mallards paddle on the ponds, and the woodlands are full of garden birds. Free entry, popular with families at weekends.
