Common Swift

普通雨燕 · 普通雨燕 · 普通雨燕 · Martinet noir · Vencejo común

Apus apus

Screaming summer visitors that fly faster than any other small bird! Swifts even SLEEP while flying, only landing to nest.

Size
Small · 17 cm
Latin name
Apus apus
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Skies above towns and cities, May to August
