Arctic Tern

北極燕鷗 · 北极燕鸥 · 北極燕鷗 · Sterne arctique · Charrán ártico

Sterna paradisaea

Champion traveller of the bird world! Flies from the Arctic to Antarctica every year — that's 70,000 km, more than any other animal!

Size
Medium · 35 cm
Latin name
Sterna paradisaea
Status
Native to Ireland
