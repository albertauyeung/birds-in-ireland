Common Crossbill

紅交嘴雀 · 红交嘴雀 · 紅交嘴雀 · Bec-croisé des sapins · Piquituerto común

Loxia curvirostra

A finch with the strangest beak in the bird world — the top and bottom CROSS over each other! Perfect for prying seeds out of pine cones.

Size
Small · 16 cm
Latin name
Loxia curvirostra
Where to spot
  • Conifer forests (Wicklow, Killarney)
  • Spruce and pine plantations
