Common Blackbird

烏鶇 · 乌鸫 · 烏鶇 · Merle noir · Mirlo común

Turdus merula

The male is jet black with a sunny orange beak. His sweet song is one of the most beautiful in any garden.

Size
Medium · 25 cm
Latin name
Turdus merula
Where to spot
  • Gardens and parks
  • Hedgerows
  • Woodland edges

  • European Stonechat (Saxicola rubicola)
  • Northern Wheatear (Oenanthe oenanthe)
  • Whinchat (Saxicola rubetra)

Where you can spot the Common Blackbird in Ireland

  • Phoenix Park — Dublin: One of the biggest city parks in Europe. Ducks on the ponds, robins in the trees, magpies on the lawns — and a herd of fallow deer too!
