Mandarin Duck

鴛鴦 · 鸳鸯 · 鴛鴦 · Canard mandarin · Pato mandarín

Aix galericulata

The most colourful duck in Ireland! The male has bright orange 'sail' feathers on his back and a fancy striped face. They came from East Asia long ago and now live wild on a few park ponds in Dublin and Wicklow.

Where you can spot the Mandarin Duck in Ireland

  • St Stephen's Green — Dublin (city centre): A leafy public park right in the middle of Dublin, a five-minute walk from Grafton Street. The big pond has Mandarin Ducks, mallards, mute swans and gulls, and the lawns are always busy with sparrows and pigeons looking for crumbs.
