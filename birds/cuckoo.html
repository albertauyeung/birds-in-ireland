Common Cuckoo

大杜鵑 · 大杜鹃 · 大杜鵑 · Coucou gris · Cuco común

Cuculus canorus

The famous bird that says "cuck-oo!" Mother cuckoos sneak their eggs into other birds' nests so other parents raise their babies!

Size
Medium · 33 cm
Latin name
Cuculus canorus
Where to spot
  • Open countryside (April–July)
  • Bogs and moors
  • Mostly heard, rarely seen
