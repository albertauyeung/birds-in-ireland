Common Sandpiper

磯鷸 · 矶鹬 · 磯鷸 · Chevalier guignette · Andarríos chico

Actitis hypoleucos

A small wader that bobs its tail up and down. It flies low over rivers with stiff, flicking wings.

Size
Small · 19 cm
Latin name
Actitis hypoleucos
Where to spot
  • River and lake edges
  • Streamsides
  • Reservoirs (summer)
