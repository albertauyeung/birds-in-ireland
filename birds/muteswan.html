Where you can spot the Mute Swan in Ireland

  • North Bull Island — Dublin: Ireland's first National Bird Sanctuary. A short bus ride from Dublin city centre — perfect for spotting waders, ducks and winter geese.
  • Wexford Wildfowl Reserve — County Wexford: Around 250 species recorded here. In winter, thousands of geese and swans gather on the wet meadows.
  • Lough Neagh — Counties Antrim/Down/Armagh/Tyrone/Derry: The biggest lake on the island of Ireland. Home to swans, ducks, terns and waders all year round.
  • St Stephen's Green — Dublin (city centre): A leafy public park right in the middle of Dublin, a five-minute walk from Grafton Street. The big pond has Mandarin Ducks, mallards, mute swans and gulls, and the lawns are always busy with sparrows and pigeons looking for crumbs.
