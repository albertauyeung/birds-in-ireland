Sand Martin

崖沙燕 · 崖沙燕 · 崖沙燕 · Hirondelle de rivage · Avión zapador

Riparia riparia

A small swallow-cousin that digs nest tunnels in sandy river banks. Hundreds nest together in cliff colonies!

Size
Small · 12 cm
Latin name
Riparia riparia
Where to spot
  • Sandy river banks
  • Quarries
  • Coastal cliffs (summer colonies)
