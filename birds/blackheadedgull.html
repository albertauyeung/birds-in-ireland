Black-headed Gull

紅嘴鷗 · 红嘴鸥 · 紅嘴鷗 · Mouette rieuse · Gaviota reidora

Chroicocephalus ridibundus

Smaller than the herring gull. In summer it has a chocolate-brown head — not actually black! In winter the head turns mostly white.

Size
Medium · 38 cm
Latin name
Chroicocephalus ridibundus
Where to spot
  • Coasts
  • Lakes
  • Parks
  • Often inland in fields

Where you can spot the Black-headed Gull in Ireland

  • North Bull Island — Dublin: Ireland's first National Bird Sanctuary. A short bus ride from Dublin city centre — perfect for spotting waders, ducks and winter geese.
