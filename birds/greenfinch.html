European Greenfinch

歐洲金翅雀 · 欧洲金翅雀 · 歐洲金翅雀 · Verdier d'Europe · Verderón común

Chloris chloris

A chunky finch with bright olive-green and yellow feathers. Loves sunflower seeds at garden feeders.

Size
Small · 15 cm
Latin name
Chloris chloris
Status
Native to Ireland
Where to spot
  • Gardens with feeders
  • Parks
  • Hedgerows
  • Woodland edges
